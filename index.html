StreetPay is a finance app for personal and business use, offering seamless vendor transactions through “Handshake” and “Nearby” features without sharing account numbers. Users can pin contacts to quickly review bills, share expenses, and allow cashiers Guest Access for transaction history. Awesome Features

Discovery for Vendors and Payees

Easily locate vendor or payee accounts with a simple toggle, and make your account discoverable for secure fund transfers using StreetPay's Handshake and Nearby features—no more need to publicly share account numbers.

Pin a Friend or Vendor

Pin up to three friends, family members, or vendors to receive bills from them directly, allowing you to enjoy services or receive bills from loved ones, such as a child in school or a partner, and conveniently review and pay.

Bill Posting

Post bills to payees or customers who have pinned you on StreetPay, enabling secure, easy billing and payments between trusted contacts.

Guest Access (for Cashiers, Partners, or Family)

Enable guest access to daily transaction history for independent verification by cashiers or partners, boosting business transparency without owner involvement. Define custom financial batches by trip, shift, or other criteria, allowing staff to track income per segment and ensure financial clarity.

Store Lister

A unique device separate from the app that interacts with both payees and payers, offering additional versatility for StreetPay users. StreetPay is crafted for total control, security, and ease in managing personal, family, and business finances.
